Overview

What is Happy Horse?

Happy Horse (HappyHorse 1.0) is a next-generation AI video generation model developed by Alibaba's ATH AI Innovation Unit, led by Zhang Di — the former technical architect of Kuaishou's Kling AI. In April 2026, Happy Horse debuted anonymously on the Artificial Analysis benchmarking platform and swiftly climbed to #1 on both the text-to-video and image-to-video leaderboards.

Built on a unified 40-layer self-attention Transformer architecture, Happy Horse generates video and audio jointly in a single forward pass. It supports native 1080p resolution output, multi-shot cinematic narratives, prompt-driven camera control, and multi-lingual lip-sync — making it one of the most capable AI video generation models available today.

Prompt Engineering

Happy Horse Prompt Writing Tips

Writing effective prompts is key to getting high-quality videos from Happy Horse. Here are community-verified practical tips.

1

Describe Camera Movements

Happy Horse natively understands cinematographic language. Use terms like "dolly in", "crane shot", "aerial dive", "slow pan" directly — they work far better than vague descriptions.

2

Specify Lighting and Mood

Concrete descriptions like "golden hour lighting", "neon reflections", "soft studio lighting" help Happy Horse precisely recreate the cinematic feel you want.

3

Include Lens Focal Lengths

Mentioning "35mm lens", "85mm portrait", "wide angle" guides Happy Horse to generate matching depth of field and perspective.

4

Leverage Multi-Shot Narratives

Describe multiple angles and scene transitions in your prompt, such as "multi-angle shots — close-up, medium, wide", to fully utilize Happy Horse's multi-shot capabilities.

5

Control Motion Pacing

Use terms like "slow motion", "time-lapse", "fluid motion" to precisely control the rhythm of your video.
