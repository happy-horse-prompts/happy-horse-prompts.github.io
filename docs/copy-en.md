## Prompt Tips
- **H2**: Happy Horse Prompt Writing Tips
- **Body**: Writing effective prompts is key to getting high-quality videos from Happy Horse. Here are community-verified practical tips:

1. **Describe camera movements** — Happy Horse natively understands cinematographic language. Use terms like "dolly in", "crane shot", "aerial dive", "slow pan" directly — they work far better than vague descriptions.
2. **Specify lighting and mood** — Concrete descriptions like "golden hour lighting", "neon reflections", "soft studio lighting" help Happy Horse precisely recreate the cinematic feel you want.
3. **Include lens focal lengths** — Mentioning "35mm lens", "85mm portrait", "wide angle" guides Happy Horse to generate matching depth of field and perspective.
4. **Leverage multi-shot narratives** — Describe multiple angles and scene transitions in your prompt, such as "multi-angle shots — close-up, medium, wide", to fully utilize Happy Horse's multi-shot capabilities.
5. **Control motion pacing** — Use terms like "slow motion", "time-lapse", "fluid motion" to precisely control the rhythm of your video.
